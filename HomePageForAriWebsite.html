Our Vision


The world is constantly evolving and changing. As we embark on advancements that adjust our future entirely, it’s crucial to keep child education in mind at all times. Our goal became to make a robot that could be viewed as a basic introduction to what technology is capable of doing, while engaging our audience in educational practices. Constructing a robot that could benefit various groups of people and serve multiple purposes had to be unique, which is where the idea of Ari came into place. Ari’s purpose is to make young children feel comfortable learning and interacting with technology. Ari is a friend that kids can actually have in the room, kids have a chance to build a personal relationship with our robot while still participating in learning.

Unlikely other forms of childhood learning, Ari is actually present with the user, developing an in person connection, allowing the user to build import relationship skills while learning, being introduced to technology. Overall, we hope that user walk away feeling as though they’ve had an enjoyable experience with Ari.
