Our Vision


The world is constantly evolving and changing. As we embark on advancements that adjust our future entirely, it’s crucial to keep child education in mind at all times. With youth education in mind, an idea for our robot flourished; our objective eventually became to make a robot that could be viewed as a basic introduction to what technology is capable of doing, while engaging our audience in educational practices. Constructing a robot that could benefit various groups of people and serve multiple purposes had to be unique, which

is where the idea of Ari came into place. Ari’s purpose is to make young children feel comfortable learning and interacting with technology.Unlike other educational outlets, Ari is actually present with the user, breaking the barrier between children and animated characters. Ari’s tangible attributes help users develop essential social skills that other educational practices lack. Users are not only getting an opportunity to learn while having fun, but also build admirable skills for the future.
